The favorites feature allows users to bookmark meals they love and optionally receive email notifications when those meals appear on the menu.

## Adding meals to favorites

You can add meals to favorites from two locations:

### From menu cards

Each meal row includes a bookmark icon on the right side:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click bookmark icon">
    Tap the empty bookmark icon next to a meal name
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="See confirmation">
    The icon fills in and a toast message confirms: "[Meal name] favorilere eklendi"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View in favorites page">
    The meal is now saved and accessible from your Favorites page
  </Step>
</Steps>

### From meal detail modal

When viewing meal details, you can also add the meal to favorites using the bookmark button in the modal.

<Note>
You must be signed in to use favorites. If you're not authenticated, clicking the bookmark icon will open a sign-in prompt.
</Note>

## Removing from favorites

To remove a meal from favorites:

**From menu cards**: Click the filled bookmark icon. It becomes empty and shows "[Meal name] favorilerden çıkarıldı"

**From favorites page**: Click the trash icon next to the meal name. The meal is removed with a confirmation toast.

## Managing favorites

Access your complete favorites list through:

- User dropdown menu → "Favorilerim"
- Direct navigation to `/favorilerim`

### Favorites page layout

The page displays:

- **Back button**: Return to the menu
- **Page title**: "Favorilerim"
- **Email notification toggle**: Enable/disable alerts (see below)
- **Favorites list**: All saved meals with action buttons

Each favorite meal shows:
- Meal name (title-cased)
- Eye icon: View meal details
- Trash icon: Remove from favorites

### Empty state

When you haven't added any favorites yet:

```
[Bookmark icon]

Henüz favori yemeğiniz yok. 
Hemen favori yemeğinizi ekleyin!

[Menüye Dön]
```

Click "Menüye Dön" to return to the menu and start adding favorites.

## Email notifications

When you have at least one favorite, an email notification card appears:

### Enabling notifications

<Steps>
  <Step title="Toggle the switch">
    Turn on the "E-posta Bildirimi" toggle at the top of your favorites list
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm email">
    The card shows your email address (from your Google account)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Receive alerts">
    You'll now get emails when any of your favorite meals appear on the menu
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Notification card details

The email notification card displays:

- **Icon**: Envelope icon indicating email feature
- **Title**: "E-posta Bildirimi"
- **Description**: "Favori yemeğiniz menüde olduğunda e-posta ([your-email]) ile bilgilendirilirsiniz."
- **Toggle switch**: On/off control

The toggle state persists across sessions and devices.

<Tip>
Email notifications are sent automatically when the daily menu is updated and contains one of your favorited meals.
</Tip>

## Visual feedback

The app provides clear visual indicators for favorited meals:

### Bookmark icon states

**Unfavorited**:
- Empty outline bookmark
- Gray color (`text-muted-foreground/40`)
- Hover: Turns red (`hover:text-red-400`)

**Favorited**:
- Filled bookmark
- Red color (`text-red-500`)
- Filled with current color
- Hover: Darker red (`hover:text-red-600`)

### Animation

When toggling favorites, the bookmark icon animates:

- Scale from 0.5 to 1.0
- Fade in/out transition
- Spring animation (stiffness: 500)
- Duration: 150ms

This smooth animation provides satisfying feedback for the user action.

## Backend synchronization

Favorites are stored in the database and synchronized across:

- **Different devices**: Access your favorites from phone, tablet, or computer
- **Different browsers**: Sign in anywhere to see your saved meals
- **App updates**: Favorites persist through app updates and cache clears

The app uses optimistic updates:
1. UI updates immediately when you click
2. Request is sent to the server in the background
3. If the server request fails, the UI reverts and shows an error toast

<Warning>
If you sign out, your favorites are preserved on the server but won't be visible until you sign in again.
</Warning>

## Use cases

Common ways students use favorites:

1. **Meal planning**: Save preferred meals to quickly find when they're available
2. **Dietary preferences**: Bookmark vegetarian or allergen-free options
3. **Novelty tracking**: Mark meals you haven't tried yet with a plan to remove them after tasting
4. **Alerts**: Enable notifications to never miss your favorite dish
5. **Recommendations**: Share favorite meal names with friends

The favorites feature turns browsing into a personalized experience tailored to individual preferences.
